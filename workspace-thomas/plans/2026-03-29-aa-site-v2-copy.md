## Direction

This version reflects a major positioning clarification:
- Arthur & Archie is not selling SaaS
- Arthur & Archie is not leading with technical architecture
- Arthur & Archie is not speaking primarily to IT buyers

The buyer is a CEO, founder, managing partner, or senior operator inside a trust-driven professional services firm.

The language should therefore emphasize:
- trust
- stewardship
- partnership
- operational clarity
- measured rollout
- accountability
- reduced risk
- seriousness over novelty

Technical definitions belong primarily inside the operating-model illustration or supporting captions, not in the main marketing copy.

### Section label
What We Do

### Headline
We deploy managed agencies for professional services firms.

### Body copy
Arthur & Archie helps professional services firms adopt agentic AI without forcing the business into a risky transformation project.

## Our Partnership

### Section label
Our Partnership

### Headline
A hands-on model built for firms where judgment matters.

### Body copy
We work in focused 3 to 5 week sprints built around a specific operational constraint.

That might be intake, qualification, follow-up, proposal flow, delivery coordination, internal handoffs, or knowledge access. We start where the firm is already feeling friction, not where the technology is most fashionable.

Each sprint is designed to produce a working improvement. We map the process, structure the right rules and context, deploy the system carefully, and expand access only as the results justify it.

Clients pay a modest onboarding fee and a term retainer. We take on the heavier implementation burden upfront so the firm does not have to make a large speculative investment before the model proves itself.

The result is not another software subscription. It is a serious operating partnership.

### Visual support intention
The process rail should visually reinforce:
- Find the constraint
- Encode the method
- Deploy carefully
- Expand on proof

Again, this should read as an engagement rhythm, not technical workflow jargon.

## The Operating Model

### Section label
The Operating Model

### Headline
Not another layer of software. A better way to run the work.

### Body copy
Most professional services firms do not need more software to log into. They need better execution across the systems, people, and knowledge they already rely on.

That is what a managed agency is designed to support.

Arthur & Archie helps firms introduce agentic systems carefully, with the right oversight, boundaries, and operational support in place. The goal is not novelty. The goal is dependable leverage in the parts of the business where context matters and mistakes are expensive.

We do not treat adoption as a one-time implementation event. We treat it as a governed operating model that earns the right to expand.

## Copy Notes

1. Keep technical language out of the main marketing body wherever possible.
2. Avoid phrases like:
   - composable language models
   - context orchestrators
   - runtime plane
   - control plane
   - agent memory/tools/skills
   unless they are constrained to the illustration or supporting technical context.
3. The buyer should feel:
   - this team understands firms like mine
   - this is serious and measured
   - this is not a gimmick
   - this is not another software product being forced on me
4. The homepage should feel like:
   - a high-trust operating partner
   - a new category expressed in familiar human terms
   - executive-safe, not technically performative
